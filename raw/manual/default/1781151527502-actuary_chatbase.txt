# Job: Actuary (Aktuari)

**All related industries:** Banking & Finance

## Key card information

- **Industry:** Banking & Finance
- **Starting Salary:** RM2800-7000
- **High Income:** Yes
- **Degree:** Degree required

**Definition:** They use maths and statistics to help companies plan financially for the future! *Mereka guna matematik dan statistik untuk membantu syarikat merancang kewangan masa depan!*

## What Is A/An Actuary?

Actuaries use maths and statistics to help companies predict how much money they might need for financial risks, unexpected events, and future expenses.

*Aktuari menggunakan matematik dan statistik untuk membantu syarikat meramalkan jumlah wang yang diperlukan bagi risiko kewangan, kejadian tidak dijangka, dan perbelanjaan masa depan.*

## Good for someone who is...

- Budak Skema  
- Budak Perform  
- Otak Bisnes  

## What Does This Job Involve?

- Actuaries perform calculations using tools like Microsoft Excel and sometimes coding.

- They apply mathematical, statistical, and financial theories to analyse data.

- They predict financial risks and advise companies on how to prepare for future uncertainties.

- Their insights help organisations make informed financial decisions.
