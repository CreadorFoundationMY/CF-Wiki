# Anisha Puri

Actuary at Allianz Malaysia. Expert interviewee featured in [[entities/Schola]]'s job guide for the [[concepts/Actuary-Career]].

## Insights (from Schola interview)

**On salary:**
- Salary increases with each actuarial exam passed, potentially adding up to RM7,000 per exam
- Earnings increase substantially after certification and experience

**On certification benefits:**
- Becoming a certified (appointed) actuary grants professional authority over financial decisions and regulatory approvals
- The Appointed Actuary title is the leadership-level role in the career hierarchy
