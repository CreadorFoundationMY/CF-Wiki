# Teachers and Counsellors

Teachers and counsellors are a target user/channel for Schola because they can help reach students at scale in trusted school contexts.

## Channel role
Schola tested whether counsellors could become a reliable channel for student reach during its 2025 scaling phase.

## Metrics cited
- 4,000 teachers.
- 2,200 counsellor subscribers.
- 14 key stakeholders.
