# Creador Foundation

**Type:** Nonprofit / foundation  
**Registration:** PPAB-01/2017 (referenced in Bahasa copyright notice)  
**Copyright notice:** © 2024 Creador Foundation / © 2025 The Creador Foundation  
**Role:** Builder and operator of [[schola]]

## Known activities
- Conducted a baseline study of 1,000+ Malaysian students on career awareness (findings used to justify Schola's creation)
- Built and maintains the Schola web platform (launched 12 August 2024)
- Runs the "Jom Jelajah Masa Depan" school outreach programme
- Produces career content (job guides, salary data, professional video testimonials)
