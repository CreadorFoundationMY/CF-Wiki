# Introduction to Schola (School X Schola)

Introductory slide deck (13 slides, English) produced by [[Creador Foundation]] presenting the [[Schola]] platform to school partners. Marked Private & Confidential, Copyright © 2024 Creador Foundation.

## Key Claims

- Schola is a **free** digital platform for career guidance targeting Malaysian secondary-school students navigating the school-to-career transition.
- Powered by [[Creador Foundation]].
- Launched **12 August 2024**.
- By post-launch (undated snapshot): **320,000+** website users, of whom **200,000+ are students**.
- **150+ requests** received from parents, teachers, and students for the school outreach programme.

## Problem Evidence

From an internal study with **1,000+ students**:
- **80%** lack essential knowledge about their chosen careers.
- **70%** do not actively research career options.
- Most students only seriously consider post-[[SPM]] pathways **after SPM results** are released.

## Features Documented

| # | Feature | Core mechanic |
|---|---------|---------------|
| 1 | **Schola Career Quiz** | [[RIASEC Holland Codes]] personality quiz → career card + job matches |
| 2 | **Subject-to-Job Match** | Select up to 2 SPM subjects → see career combinations |
| 3 | **Job Guides** | Per-job: description, salary data, education path, expert advice, future outlook, knowledge quiz |
| 4 | **Gaji Goals Game** | Gamified 10-year salary simulation; compare two careers side-by-side |

## School Programme (Jom Jelajah Masa Depan Bersama Schola)

Duration: **1.5 hours**

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 10 min | Schola & FIF Introduction |
| 25 min | Activity 1 — Bingo: Kawan Baru, Kerjaya Baru |
| 20 min | Website Exploration |
| 30 min | Activity 2 — Reality Check Kerjaya |
| 5 min | Feedback Survey |

Programme is flexible; schools can incorporate their own activities.

## Core Belief

> "You can't be what you don't know."
> Everyone has talent and potential, but not everyone has the same access to opportunities.
