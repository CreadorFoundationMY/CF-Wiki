# Schola Retrieval Brief

## One-liner
Schola is a free Malaysian secondary-student career guidance platform focused on early career exposure, education pathways, and informed post-school decisions.

## Agent-ready facts
- Launch date stated in deck: 12 August 2024.
- Reported reach since launch: 540,000+ unique users, 315,000+ students, 200+ schools, 40,000+ programme-engaged students.
- Research base: about 1,000 student surveys and 50 in-depth interviews.
- Key problem: students delay career research and rely on familiarity, producing narrow aspirations and weak pathway understanding.
- Product metrics from 2024-2025 changes: homepage feature adoption 5% to 37%; directory-to-guide conversion 37% to 54%; job-guide tab exploration 2 to 5 tabs; content interaction 65% to 84%.
- Ecosystem engines: social media, programmes, product, content, data/analytics.
- 2026 direction: retention, product depth, complete student journey, teacher/counsellor/school-system adoption, and national education-priority alignment.
