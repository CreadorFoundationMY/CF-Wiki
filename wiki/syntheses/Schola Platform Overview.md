# Schola Platform Overview

Synthesised picture of [[Schola]] as a career-guidance intervention for Malaysian secondary-school students, drawn from the introductory pitch deck (School X Schola, 2024).

## What It Is

A free, web-based platform (schola.org.my) operated by [[Creador Foundation]] that helps Malaysian students explore careers before — not after — SPM. Launched 12 August 2024.

## Problem → Solution Logic

```
Problem: Students have ambitions but lack career knowledge & exploration habits
  → 80% lack knowledge about chosen careers
  → 70% don't actively research
  → Most plan post-SPM pathways only AFTER SPM

Solution: Free, engaging, self-directed platform that makes career exploration
accessible and fun DURING secondary school
```

## Feature Architecture

| Layer | Feature | Addresses |
|-------|---------|-----------|
| Self-discovery | Career Quiz (RIASEC) | "Who am I?" |
| Exploration | Subject-to-Job Match | "What could I become given what I study?" |
| Deep research | Job Guides | "What does this job actually involve?" |
| Financial literacy | Gaji Goals Game | "Will this career sustain the life I want?" |

The four features form a funnel: broad personality assessment → subject-anchored discovery → job-level research → financial scenario planning.

## Go-to-School Programme

[[Creador Foundation]] deploys a 1.5-hour facilitated school session ("Jom Jelajah Masa Depan Bersama Schola") that integrates platform use with structured group activities (career bingo, reality-check exercise).

## Traction Snapshot

As of the undated snapshot (post-Aug 2024):
- 320,000+ total users
- 200,000+ student users
- 150+ inbound requests from schools/parents/teachers

## Key Design Choices

- **Free access**: removes cost as a barrier for underserved students.
- **Bilingual (BM + EN)**: meets students where they are.
- **RIASEC-grounded**: anchors recommendations in validated occupational psychology.
- **Salary transparency**: shows real salary bands (sourced from practitioners + official reports) to make career stakes concrete.
- **Future outlook section**: prepares students for changing labour market conditions.
