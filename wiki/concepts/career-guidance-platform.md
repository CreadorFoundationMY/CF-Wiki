# Career Guidance Platform

**Type:** Product category  
**Primary example in this wiki:** [[schola]]

## Definition

A career guidance platform is a digital tool that helps students or job-seekers:
- Discover careers they may not be aware of
- Assess personal interests and aptitudes relative to career options
- Understand the educational pathways, salary ranges, and day-to-day realities of specific roles
- Make informed decisions about their educational and professional trajectory

## Schola's Approach

[[schola]] combines four distinct interaction modes to cover the full guidance funnel:

| Feature | Guidance Function |
|---------|-------------------|
| Career Quiz (RIASEC) | Self-assessment → personality-matched career discovery |
| Subject-to-Job Match | Anchor on existing school context → expand career awareness |
| Job Guides | Deep-dive on specific roles — salary, pathway, expert voice |
| Gaji Goals Game | Long-horizon financial comparison — motivate early planning |

This layered approach addresses students at different stages: those with no career idea (quiz), those anchored to school subjects (subject match), and those researching a known career (job guides).

## Key Design Principles (from source)

- **Free** — removes financial access barriers
- **Mobile-first** — UI shown as smartphone app/web
- **Bilingual** — content available in English (EN) and Bahasa Malaysia (BM)
- **Evidence-based** — quiz methodology (RIASEC) and subject-matching tool developed from student research
- **Expert-grounded** — salary data from professionals and official sources; tips from real practitioners
