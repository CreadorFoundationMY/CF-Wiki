# Schola Product Evolution

Schola's product development moved from early prototypes to a 2024 go-live and 2025 engagement improvements.

## Timeline
- Apr 2022: Prototype 1.0 focused on self-awareness guides and worksheets.
- Sept 2022: Prototype 2.0 added pre-university write-ups and funding search.
- Jun 2023: MVP 1.0 included pre-university matching, industry overviews, and TVET field-of-interest matching.
- Aug 2023: Prototype 3.0 deepened industry and job exploration, funding search, and income-based matching.
- July 2024: Pilot Phase 1 included interest-based career matching, comparison tools, job write-ups, and education planning guides.
- Aug 2024: Website go-live.

## 2024-2025 product changes
- Homepage revamp increased feature adoption from 5% to 37%.
- Directory revamp increased conversion to a job guide from 37% to 54%.
- Job guides improved from 2 to 5 explored tabs and content interaction from 65% to 84%.
- Design shifted toward multiple entry points and industry-centric exploration.
