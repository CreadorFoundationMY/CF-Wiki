# Product Glow-Up 2024-2025

Schola's 2024-2025 product changes focused on helping students move beyond a singular lens of career exploration and understand industries, job categories, salaries, and expert perspectives.

## Reported improvements
- Homepage revamp: feature adoption increased from 5% to 37%.
- Directory revamp: conversion to a job guide increased from 37% to 54%.
- Job guides: average explored tabs increased from 2 to 5 out of 5; content interaction increased from 65% to 84%.

## Product moves
- Shifted the main call-to-action toward students who already have ambitions.
- Created multiple entry points for exploration.
- Moved toward industry-centric exploration.
- Reused a tab/filter design across job guides and funding tools.
- Improved job category tagging by the content team.
- Changed job guides to address shallow salary understanding, low visibility, weak enticement, and relevance gaps.

## Development timeline
- Apr 2022: Prototype 1.0.
- Sept 2022: Prototype 2.0.
- Jun 2023: MVP 1.0.
- Aug 2023: Prototype 3.0.
- July 2024: Pilot Phase 1.
- Aug 2024: website go-live.
